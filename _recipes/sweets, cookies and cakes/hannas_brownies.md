---
title: Hanna's Brownies
description: Amazing brownies with great texture and a nice hint of coffee
ingredients:
- title:
  steps:
  - 57 g butter, melted
  - 100 g granulated sugar
  - 50 g muscavado sugar
  - 40 g milk
  - 1/8 tsp salt
  - 2 tsp vanilla sugar
  - 1 tsp instant coffee powder
  - 71 g flour
  - 32 g cocoa powder
  - 1/2 tsp baking powder
  - 100 g chopped chocolate (I like to mix dark and white)
instructions:
- title:
  steps:
  - Set the oven to 175°C
  - Prepare a smaller baking pan lined with baking paper
  - Mix melted butter, sugar, milk, salt, vanilla sugar and instant cofee powder in a bowl
  - Sift and mix flour, cocoa powder and baking powder in a separet bowl
  - Mix the two bowls, and stir in the chopped chocolate
  - Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30-35 minutes in the bottom of the oven. The surface should look a bit dry and cracked.

yield: 6 portions

total_time: 50 min
