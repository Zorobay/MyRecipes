---
title: The 1910
description: Adapted from a recipe created in 2014 by Ezra Star at Drink in Boston, USA, this Martinez riff is named after the year the half-decade long The Mexican Revolution (Mexican Civil War) started. This had a transformative effect on both Mexican culture and government.
ingredients:
    - title:
    - steps: 
      - 22.5 ml Mezcal
      - 22.5 ml Cognac
      - 15 ml Luxardo Maraschino
      - 30 ml Punt E Mes Vermouth
      - 2 dashes Peychaud's Bitters
instructions:
  - title:
    steps:
    - Stir ingredients with ice
    - Strain into chilled cocktail glass
    - Garnish with Maraschino cherry

yield: 1 cocktail
