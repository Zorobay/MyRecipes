---
title: Easy Speak
description: Similar to a Manhattan or an Old Fashioned, but a bit easier to drink! Really nice if you want something boozy!
ingredients:
  - title:
    steps:
    - 60 ml Bourbon whiskey
    - 22.g ml Cynar
    - 10 ml Rich Syrup (2:1)
    - 1 dash Chocolate bitters
instructions:
  - title:
    steps:
    - Combine the ingredients
    - Stir with ice until chilled
    - Strain over ice into old fashioned glass
    - Garnish with a twist of lemon
image: easy_speak.jpg
yield: 1 cocktail
category: cocktails
---
